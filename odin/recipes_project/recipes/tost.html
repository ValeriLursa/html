French toast

Description

French toast - delicious and quick breakfast


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 15g butter
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 100ml milk
  • 3t.s. sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with sugar.
  2. Mix with milk.
  3. Melt the butter in a frying pan.
  4. Dip the bread into the egg mixture.
  5. Place the bread slices in the skillet and cook over medium heat until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes on each side.
  6. Serve with honey or syrup.
