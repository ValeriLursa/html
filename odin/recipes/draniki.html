Description

Draniki made of potatoes, the recipe with a photo of which I offer below, is a popular hot snack in the countries of Eastern Europe. But in order to cook draniki deliciously, you need to know some subtleties of the process.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of potatoes
  • 2 pcs. onion
  • 2 pcs. an egg
  • 2 Art. l. flour
  • 1 Art. l. sour cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt to taste
  • spices to taste
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. How to make potato pancakes in a pan? Prepare the ingredients. Take a deep frying pan with a thick bottom and a lid. Wash potatoes, peel and grate on a medium grater. If desired, it is fashionable to squeeze the minced potato from the juice, rinse from excess starch, then the potato pancakes will turn out to be snow-white. But it is better not to do this, then potato pancakes will be more tasty, crispy and ruddy.
  2. Place the minced potato in a deep bowl. Add salt and black pepper to it to taste. Beat in one chicken egg.
  3. Stir the mass so that the chicken egg and spices mix with the minced potato.
  4. Add flour to potato mixture. Add it with a spoon in parts, mixing well each time to monitor the consistency of the dough.
  5. It should turn out quite thick, thicker than sour cream. Then pancakes will turn out plump and lush. Pour vegetable oil into a frying pan, heat it up.
  6. Spoon one tablespoon of the potato batter onto the pancake, smoothing the edges to keep the batter from spreading. When the potato dough sets well, forming a good crust on the bottom, cover the pan with a lid, make the fire medium and fry for about 10 minutes. Then remove the lid, fry the potato pancakes for about 5 minutes, then turn over and fry until golden brown on the second side. So fry each batch of potato pancakes.
  7. Remove them carefully with a wooden spatula on a plate covered with a napkin so that the remaining vegetable oil drains. Serve pancakes to the table with sour cream and fresh vegetables and herbs. Enjoy your meal!
